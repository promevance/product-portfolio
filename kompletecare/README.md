# KompleteCare — B2B Growth & Go-To-Market Portfolio

This repository documents my work on **B2B growth, lead generation, and go-to-market execution** at KompleteCare, a healthcare technology marketplace operating across hospitals, HMOs, laboratories, pharmacies, and healthcare providers.

The focus of this portfolio is not design or code, but **how growth was driven**, **the systems used**, and **the business outcomes achieved**.

KompleteCare is a healthcare marketplace that connects:

While the platform initially leaned toward B2C telemedicine, my most impactful work focused on **B2B adoption**, specifically:

## Core Problem We Solved

Hospitals and HMOs struggled with:
- Slow claim approvals  
- Manual, fragmented communication  
- Delayed payments affecting patient care  

We identified a major opportunity in fixing **hospital–HMO workflows**, rather than focusing only on patient consultations.

This insight led to the launch of a B2B-focused product that became a major revenue driver.

---

## Strategy Overview

### 1. Lead Magnet–Driven B2B Acquisition

Instead of cold-selling software, we:
- Created educational and operational **lead magnets**
- Targeted decision-makers already experiencing the problem
- Positioned KompleteCare as infrastructure, not just a tool

Lead magnets included:
- Workflow explainers
- Operational guides
- Product walkthroughs tailored to hospital and HMO use cases

---

### 2. Channel Strategy

Primary acquisition channels:
- Google Ads (search intent driven)
- LinkedIn outreach to hospital and HMO stakeholders
- Direct follow-up and demos
- Referral incentives post-activation

The focus was **quality over volume**.

---

### 3. Qualification and Conversion

Once a stakeholder engaged with a lead magnet:
- Contact details were captured
- I handled direct follow-ups via calls and demos
- Stakeholders were onboarded to a trial
- Teams were trained to use the platform
- Trials were converted into paid usage

This process was deliberate and hands-on.
